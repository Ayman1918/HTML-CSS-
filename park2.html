AYUB NATIONAL PARK

THE BEST AMUSEMENT PARK

Ayub National Park, commonly known as Ayub Park or, historically, Topi Rakh Park , is a national park located on the Grand Trunk Road, not far away from the old presidency in Rawalpindi, Punjab,Pakistan. The park was established before the creation of Pakistan and covers an area of 313 acres.
