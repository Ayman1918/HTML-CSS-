National Park Info

On post 25 March 2023

Our national parks offer visitors a unique opportunity to explore some of the most stunning and diverse landscapes in the world. From the rugged mountains of the Himalayas to the lush green forests of the south, our parks are home to a wide range of wildlife, including rare and endangered species such as snow leopards, Himalayan brown bears, and Indus river dolphins. At National Parks of Pakistan, we are committed to promoting sustainable tourism and ensuring that visitors have a positive impact on the environment and local communities. We work closely with local communities to develop ecotourism programs that support conservation efforts and provide economic opportunities for residents.
